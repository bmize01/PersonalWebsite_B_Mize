Welcome to Brianna's Website

Hello! I am happy to see you stop by. Please take this website with a grain of salt since it is my first time ever designing a website on Adobe Dreamweaver.

On this website we have 5 pages that have different content relating back to my art practice! My brand name is Bee Rose Imagery. I create art across the board. I focus on photography and poetry. However, I am a jack of all trades. I have dipped my toes in painting, non-fiction writing, papercutting, sewing, crocheting, or anything else that might fit into the creative arts practices.

 

Overview

I will be using this website to talk about what I do, and why I care about it. Secondly, I am including a portfolio to display my photography in which are completed photographs from different photo sessions that I have had recently. My current projects will function similarly to the portfolio, but we are going to talk more about different art forms separate from photography and what I am working on and what I hope to do in the future. Finally, on our last page there is an opportunity to send me an email for inquiries and questions about what I am doing! I hope to hear from you!
